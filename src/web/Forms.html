  • L.A. 1(B) Claim for Fees - (Barrister only, District Court or an Appeal to the Circuit Court)
Claim for fees (barrister only, District Court or an appeal to the Circuit Court) in circumstances where counsel has been assigned by the Court under the Non-Statutory District Court (Counsel) Scheme.
  • L.A. 1 (Debtors) Claim for Fees (Solicitor Only, District Court or an appeal to the Circuit Court) and Solicitors Travelling
Claim for fees (solicitor only, District Court or an appeal to the Circuit Court) and solicitors travelling and subsistence expenses in circumstances where a debtor has been granted a legal aid certificate under the Enforcement of Court Orders Act 1940 (as amended by the Enforcement of Court Orders (Amendment) Act 2009).
  • Claim for Allowances by a Witness who Attended Court on Behalf of the Defence
L.A. 2 - Claim for allowances by a witness who was summoned and attended court on behalf of the defence to give evidence as to fact at the hearing of a case in relation to which a certificate for free legal aid has been granted
  • Claim for Fee For Medical or Technical Report
L.A. 5 - Claim for a fee for a medical or technical report required by the defence in a case where a certificate for free legal aid has been granted
  • Claim for Fees and Expenses by a Witness holding a Professional Qualification
L.A. 3 - Claim for fees and expenses by a witness holding a professional qualification, whether medical or otherwise, who was summoned and attended court on behalf of the defence to give professional evidence at the hearing of a case in relation to which a certificate for free legal aid has been granted
  • Criminal Legal Aid Form (Solicitors Expenses)
L.A. 10 - A claim form for payment of a solicitor's expenses
